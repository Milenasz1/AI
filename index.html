Artificial Intelligence



AI is a field of study and development that focuses on creating systems and programs capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence. These systems use algorithms and mathematical models to analyze data, learn from it, make decisions and take actions based on established goals.

Autonomous Driving

If the electric car is the future, autonomous driving is the next link in the chain of evolution of the automotive sector. Autonomous driving is a driving mode that consists of operating the vehicle without the active control of a driver when its technology is active.
An autonomous vehicle is considered as such when it is equipped with sufficient technology to allow its operation without actively requiring the control or supervision of a driver.
